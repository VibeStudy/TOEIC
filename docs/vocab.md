## 자동사 vs 타동사 (수동태 판단 기준)

> **핵심 원칙**: 타동사만 수동태(be + p.p.)로 쓸 수 있다.
> 자동사는 목적어를 받지 않으므로 수동태 불가.

### 자동사 (수동태 ❌)

| 동사 | 뜻 | 예문 |
|------|----|------|
| arise | 발생하다 | An issue **arose** during the final review that required input from the legal team. |
| occur | 발생하다 | The billing error **occurred** due to a system glitch during the software update. |
| happen | 일어나다 | The power outage **happened** without warning, interrupting the afternoon presentations. |
| appear | 나타나다, ~처럼 보이다 | A promising solution **appeared** after the team reviewed the original design specifications. |
| disappear | 사라지다 | Several key files **disappeared** from the shared drive after the server was migrated. |
| remain | 여전히 ~이다, 남다 | The office will **remain** closed on public holidays throughout the year. |
| rise | 오르다 | Export volumes **rose** steadily during the third quarter. |
| fall | 떨어지다 | Profit margins **fell** slightly due to an increase in raw material costs. |
| lie | 놓여 있다 | The original blueprints **lie** in the archive room on the second floor. |
| exist | 존재하다 | A formal grievance procedure already **exists** for employees to raise concerns. |
| consist of | ~로 구성되다 | The inspection committee **consists of** two engineers and an independent auditor. |
| result from | ~에서 비롯되다 | The project delay **resulted from** unexpected complications during the approval process. |
| result in | ~을 야기하다 | Poor inventory management **resulted in** a significant loss of perishable goods. |
| belong to | ~에 속하다 | The signed documents **belong to** the legal department and must not be removed. |
| last | 지속되다 | The product demonstration **lasted** approximately 45 minutes, followed by a Q&A session. |

토익에서 수동태로 자주 출제되는 동사 위주.

| 동사 | 뜻 | 수동태 예문 |
|------|----|------------|
